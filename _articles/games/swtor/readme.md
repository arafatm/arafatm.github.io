# Classes

[Vulkk Lethality guide](https://vulkk.com/2019/12/03/swtor-6-0-lethality-operative-guide/)
- **1590** for 110% **Accuracy**;
- **1213+** for 1st **Alacrity** Threshold / 3208+ for 2nd Alacrity Threshold;
- Everything else put in **Critical** Rating;
- The best Mods on 6.0, at the moment, are the unlettered R-2, unlettered R-3
  and unlettered R-5 but unlettered and unnumbered version work just fine;
- The best Armorings, Barrels and Hilts are the unlettered and unnumbered and
  unlettered R-1 versions.
