## 

Inline formulas enclose `\( ... \)`

Displayed formulas `\[ ... \]`

Curly braces `{}` to group pieces of formulas

Superscripts (`^`) and subscripts (`_`) : $`{x_1}^e`$

### Fractions:

$\frac {a} {b}$ \\frac {a} {b}

${ {x} \over {y} }$ {x} \\over {y} }
